DIRECTIONS TO OLYMPIA C.U.S.D. #16

ADMINISTRATION BUILDING, HIGH SCHOOL AND MIDDLE SCHOOL


From I-55 North or South taking Shirley Exit (Bloomington, Springfield)
At ramp stop sign, turn west. At next stop sign (Road 1075E) turn left. At Shirley sign (Road 850N), turn right. Go over bridge, stay on 850N approximately 6 miles. At next stop sign (Road 500E, T-road) turn left. Take first road to the right (Road 800N, McLean County 34). Olympia is approximately 4 miles west on the south side of the road. Look for blue water tower. The Olympia Unit Office building is to the northwest of the Olympia High/Middle School building across a parking lot. Parking is on the south and east side of the Unit Office building.

From Bloomington, IL, taking Route 9
Take Route 9 west. Continue straight, road becomes Route 122 (don't turn toward Danvers and follow Route 9). At curve, follow the curve, don't go straight. Continue on Route 122 until you reach the Stanford road. Turn left into Stanford. Go through Stanford and on about 4 miles to a stop sign at a T road. Turn right (west) and continue until you reach the school on your left. Look for the blue water tower.

From Peoria, IL
Interstate 74 east to Interstate 155 (Lincoln exit) and follow to Hopedale exit (Route 122 east) and follow to Minier, then turn right (south) through Minier and on for 3 miles to Olympia Road. Turn left (east) and go approximately 3 miles. The Olympia Unit Office is the first steel building, turn into the Olympia High/Middle School parking lot and parking is on the south or east side of the building.

From Champaign, IL
Interstate 74 west to Route 9 (Pekin exit). Follow Route 9 west; it will split into Route 122. Stay on Route 122 west to Stanford. Turn left (south), go through Stanford, and proceed (south) for approximately 3 miles. At stop sign at the T road, turn right for 1.5 miles. The Olympia Unit Office will be on your left just past the Olympia High/Middle School building (metal building/bus garage). Parking is on the south side of the building.

From Springfield, IL, through Mclean, IL
Interstate 55 (north) to the 145 mile marker, town of McLean. At bottom of ramp, turn left on Route 136 West through McLean to County Road 400E. Turn right and follow through double S curve. Turn right on County Road 59N. Follow to County Road 800N, turn left, and proceed 2 miles west. The Olympia Unit Office building will be on the west side of the Olympia High/Middle School building. Parking is on the south and east side of the building.

Coming west on Olympia Road: The high school entrance is the first road; The middle school and the unit office are the third entrance. The Unit Office is the metal building across from the football field. There are signs indicating the buildings.
